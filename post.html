Steph Curry

Stephen Curry, often called Steph Curry, is a professional basketball player. He plays for the Golden State Warriors in the NBA. He is known for his incredible shooting skills, especially three-pointers. Curry was born on March 14, 1988, in Akron, Ohio. He played college basketball for Davidson College, where he gained attention for leading his team to the NCAA Tournament's Final Four. Steph Curry joined the Golden State Warriors in 2009, and since then, he has become one of the greatest shooters in NBA history. He helped the Warriors win NBA championships in 2015, 2017, and 2018. Curry is famous for his unique playing style and incredible long-range shooting. His accurate three-point shots have changed the way basketball is played. Off the court, he's known for his philanthropy work and positive influence. In summary, Steph Curry is a basketball superstar known for his amazing shooting skills, particularly his three-pointers, and he has played a key role in the success of the Golden State Warriors
